# LOOMWIRE Showcase

LOOMWIRE is positioned as:

> Shopify helps you sell. Canva helps you design. Notion helps you organize.
> LOOMWIRE helps you turn an idea into a protected cultural universe.

## MVP Flow

1. Enter a raw brand idea.
2. Choose industry, audience, style, target, product blank, mood, price level,
   and long-term vision.
3. Pick an AI lane: demo, OpenAI, Claude, OpenRouter, Groq, or Ollama.
4. Generate a complete brand system.
5. Export the evidence JSON.

## Strong First Use Case

Input:

```text
I want to build a luxury streetwear brand inspired by African futurism and AI culture.
```

Output:

- Brand name options.
- Brand philosophy.
- Logo direction.
- Trademark preparation checklist.
- Moodboard and palette language.
- Product drop idea.
- Lookbook outline.
- Website hero copy.
- Launch plan.
- Social bio.
- Brand DNA Score.
- AI Brand Critic notes.

## Original Visual Direction

The site uses original generated artwork inspired by street-art posters, gallery
installations, fashion archives, yellow evidence paper, signal green, black-box
labs, and cultural product drops.
