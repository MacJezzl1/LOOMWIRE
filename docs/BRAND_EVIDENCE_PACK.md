# Brand Evidence Pack

The Brand Evidence Pack is a practical record of authorship and launch
preparation. It is not legal advice. It gives a creator a clean folder of facts,
dates, files, and decisions to bring to an IP professional or business partner.

## Suggested Folder

```text
brand-evidence-pack/
  01-idea-summary.md
  02-name-shortlist.md
  03-final-name-rationale.md
  04-logo-and-art-direction.md
  05-product-blank.md
  06-lookbook-export.pdf
  07-launch-page-screenshot.png
  08-social-and-domain-checks.md
  09-contributor-records.md
  10-first-use-notes.md
```

## What To Record

- First written idea and author.
- Name options, meanings, rejection notes, and final choice.
- Logo prompts, drafts, source files, exports, and creator notes.
- Product drop concept, supplier research, pricing logic, and mockups.
- Domain, social handle, and marketplace availability research.
- Lookbook export, launch page screenshot, and campaign calendar.
- Contributor agreements, licenses, and source links.

## LOOMWIRE Principle

Protect the idea before the world touches it.
